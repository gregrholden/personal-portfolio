About Me

I'm a professional writer and editor who transitioned into tech. I have a degree in software development, a decade of experience on the content and marketing side of websites and social media, and a diverse set of skills ranging across entire stacks. I am university trained in Java SE8 and EE7 (JSF, JDBC, servlets) and relational databases (primarily Oracle and MySQL), and self-taught in web development, specializing in core web tech: HTML5/CSS3/JS as well as automated tooling with Gulp, version control with Git/GitHub, modern stylesheet management with PostCSS, and modern HTML markup with Pug. My current project is to learn and master React.
